About Us

AI Church Broadcast

Empowering communication through intelligent automation.

This platform leverages advanced Artificial Intelligence to transform live speech into meaningful, structured content in real time. It is designed to automatically detect and extract Bible verses from sermons, display dynamic on-screen overlays, and generate intelligent, well-organized notes that users can easily download and reference.

Beyond religious settings, the application is built with versatility in mind. It can be effectively deployed across conferences, seminars, educational institutions, media houses, and corporate organizations. By converting spoken content into accurate text-based records, the platform enhances documentation, improves accessibility, and ensures that important information is never lost.

Whether used for spiritual engagement, academic learning, professional events, or content production, this solution empowers users to capture, manage, and share knowledge efficiently. Our goal is to bridge the gap between spoken communication and digital documentation through intelligent automation.

The platform may use microphone access for live speech capture and internet connectivity for services such as scripture lookup and connected integrations. Users should review the privacy and terms pages for data usage, user rights, and service disclaimers. Developed by Samuel Olasunkanmi Abatan.
