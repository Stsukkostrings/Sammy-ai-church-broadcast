OmniCast AI

Empowering organizations through intelligent communication and media automation.

OmniCast AI transforms live speech into structured, useful content in real time. It helps teams capture spoken sessions, generate notes and summaries, prepare overlays, record audio, and manage content for distribution across digital channels.

The platform is designed for churches, schools, universities, NGOs, businesses, government institutions, event organizers, and media teams. By turning live communication into searchable and reusable media assets, OmniCast AI improves documentation, accessibility, coordination, and audience reach.

In addition to live capture and broadcast support, the platform can support media publishing workflows, scheduled content distribution, and social sharing. Specialized faith-based tools such as scripture assistance can remain available where needed, while the overall platform serves a much wider professional audience.

The platform may use microphone access for live speech capture and internet connectivity for connected services, publishing integrations, and content lookup features. Users should review the privacy and terms pages for data usage, user rights, and service disclaimers. Developed by Sammy G Concept.
