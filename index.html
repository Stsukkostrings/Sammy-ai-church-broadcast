About The Product

A communication and media workspace built for live capture, planning, publishing, and audience engagement.

This web app mirrors the Android experience while staying lightweight enough for production desks, communications teams, presenters, and field operators using a laptop or browser.

What It Does

  • Continuous mic listening in supported browsers
  • Editable live session notes with download and share
  • Session audio recording with download support
  • Overlay and presentation preparation tools
  • Session archive for reusable history
  • Planner for live production execution
  • AI-ready summaries and promo snippets
  • Optional scripture and faith-study tools
  • Foundation for social and media publishing workflows

Best Use Cases

  • Church and faith media teams
  • Corporate communications departments
  • Schools, universities, and training events
  • NGOs and advocacy organizations
  • Conference and event production crews
  • Studios, livestream teams, and media desks
